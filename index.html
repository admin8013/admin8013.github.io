Frequently Asked Question?

  • What is BIKINI?

    +

    BIKINI is a flexible supply token built on bsc, which aims to increase by 9.81% every 8 hours by repricing every 4 hours.

  • What is a flexible supply token?

    +

    Elastic supply makes the total supply of tokens change. This is a mechanism designed to keep prices rising. The number of tokens will eventually deflate to 1, and this token will be the most expensive token in the world.

  • What is redefinition?

    +

    Imagine that there is a flexible supply token here. We want it to maintain the value of $1. If the price is less than $1, the redefinition will reduce the total supply of tokens, thereby increasing the value of each token.

  • Why will my tokens decrease?

    +

    If you have 2 tokens, each of which is 1 USD, and when you own 2 tokens. The market value is 100 USD and you own 2%. After the redefinition, there are a total of 50 tokens, and you own one, but the price is $2. You still own 2 dollars worth of tokens, and you still own 2%. If the market value rises to $200, your investment will double

  • How to judge my investment income?

    When you become a BIK holder, you will have a certain percentage of the market share. As the redefinition occurs, the number of tokens in your wallet will decrease, but the percentage of the total market share you own will never change (assuming you don't buy or sell). As the market value increases, the value of your investment will also increase
